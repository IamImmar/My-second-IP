Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. This website takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born depending on their gender outputs.


The names are:

Male:Sunday: Kwasi, Monday: Kwadao, Tuesday: Kwabena, Wednesday: Kwaku, Thursday: Yaw, Friday: Kofi, Saturday: Kwame

Female:Sunday: Akosua, Monday: Adwoa, Tuesday: Abenaa, Wednesday: Akua, Thursday: Yaa, Friday: Afua, Saturday: Ama
